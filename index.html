The Real Problem

Brands lose at the shelf.
And they rarely know why.

Field teams still report via WhatsApp and spreadsheets. Shelf gaps go unnoticed. Promo displays are inconsistently deployed. Management has no real-time view across hundreds of outlets.

📋

Manual, Unverifiable Reporting

Field teams send photos via WhatsApp with no standard format, no timestamp proof, and no traceability.

📦

Out-of-Stocks Go Undetected

Without real-time shelf monitoring, OOS events are only discovered on the next scheduled visit — often 3 to 7 days later.

🎯

Promotions Not Verified

Brands spend on POSM and activation campaigns but have no proof of display placement or promoter attendance.

📊

Zero Management Visibility

Management receives reports days after the fact. No single source of truth for outlet performance or field team productivity.
